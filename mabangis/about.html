About Us

We are a small team of passionate developers and designers based in the Philippines. We love building things for the web and we are always learning something new.

Who We Are

A Small Team with Big Ideas

TRIO is a three-person creative studio made up of two full-stack developers and a UI/UX researcher. We started this studio because we wanted to work on projects we actually care about — not just assignments, but real things that help real people.

We met in college and realized pretty quickly that our skills complement each other really well. One of us loves coding the backend, one focuses on the front-end and design, and one makes sure everything we build actually makes sense to the people using it.

We are still learning every day but we are proud of what we have built so far.
