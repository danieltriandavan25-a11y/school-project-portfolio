How We Work

Our Process

We follow a simple and clear process for every project. No surprises, no guesswork — just a step by step approach that gets things done right.

Step by Step

From Idea to Launch

Every project we take on follows the same process. It keeps things organized and makes sure both we and the client are always on the same page.

01
Discovery

Understanding the Project

Before anything else, we sit down and talk. We ask questions, listen carefully, and make sure we fully understand what the client wants to build and why. We look at any references or inspiration the client has, and we clarify the goals, timeline, and budget. This step is the most important one because everything else builds on it.

📋 Project brief and requirements gathering
🎯 Goal setting and scope definition
🕐 Timeline and milestone planning
Estimated: 1 — 2 days
02
Research

Research and Planning

Once we know what we are building, we do our research. Harvey leads this phase — we look at competitors, interview target users if needed, and analyze what already exists in the space. We identify pain points and opportunities. Then we plan the structure of the product, including the sitemap, user flows, and feature list.

🔍 Competitor and market research
👥 User interviews and surveys
🗺️ Sitemap and user flow creation
Estimated: 2 — 4 days
03
Design

Wireframing and UI Design

With the research done, Harvey moves into design. We start with low-fidelity wireframes to map out the layout and structure without worrying about colors or details yet. Once the wireframes are approved, we move to high-fidelity designs in Figma — complete with colors, typography, spacing, and components. The client reviews and approves before we touch any code.

✏️ Low-fidelity wireframes
🎨 High-fidelity UI design in Figma
✅ Client review and design approval
Estimated: 3 — 5 days
04
Development

Building the Product

This is where Daniel and Kherby take over. We translate the approved designs into actual code. We build the front-end first, making sure everything looks exactly like the design and works on all screen sizes. Then we build the back-end — the database, server logic, user authentication, and any other features the project needs. We use Git to track our progress and keep the code organized.

💻 Front-end development
⚙️ Back-end and database development
🔧 Version control with Git
Estimated: 5 — 14 days
05
Testing

Testing and Fixing

Before we show anything to the client, we test it ourselves. We check every page, every button, every form, and every feature. We test on different browsers and screen sizes. If something looks wrong or does not work properly, we fix it before the client ever sees it. We also do a quick usability check to make sure everything makes sense from a user perspective.

🧪 Functional and browser testing
📱 Mobile and responsive testing
🐛 Bug fixing and polish
Estimated: 2 — 3 days
06
Launch

Delivery and Launch

The final step. We present the finished product to the client, walk them through how everything works, and make any last adjustments. Once everything is approved, we hand over the files, deploy if needed, and the project goes live. We stay available for a short period after launch to handle any issues that come up.

🚀 Final client presentation and approval
📦 File handover and deployment
🤝 Post-launch support
Estimated: 1 — 2 days
